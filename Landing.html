How it works

Up and running in minutes

RADevo is designed to slot seamlessly into your existing teaching — no complicated setup, no training days, no IT tickets.

1

Create your account

Sign in with your school Google account. Add your classes, select your subject and exam board, and you're ready to go.

2

Set homework or assignments

Choose a quiz from the content library and set it for your class in seconds. Students get an email notification and a direct link.

3

Students complete quizzes

Four activity types per quiz — Fill in the Blanks, Match Keywords, Multiple Choice and True or False — all adaptive to each student's weak areas.

4

Track progress instantly

Your teacher dashboard shows every student's score across every activity. AI analysis identifies class-wide weak spots and suggests next steps.

5

Run live in-lesson assignments

Start a timed in-class assignment with one click. A QR code appears for your projector, students scan and begin — you watch results arrive in real time.

6

Grades improve

Regular low-stakes retrieval practice, backed by adaptive difficulty, helps students retain content and walk into exams with confidence.

Features

Everything your department needs

Built by teachers, for teachers. Every feature is designed to save time and have a direct impact on student outcomes.

🧠

Adaptive Difficulty

RADevo remembers which questions students got wrong and weights them higher in future sessions. More practice where it matters most.

📡

Live Assignment Monitoring

See exactly who has started, who is in progress and who has submitted — in real time. Perfect for in-lesson tasks.

✨

AI-Powered Analysis

One click generates a detailed class report — top performers, weak topics, question-level insights and suggested teaching actions.

📋

Homework Management

Set homework, track submissions, send automatic reminders for overdue work, and view per-student scores — all from one page.

📱

QR Code Sharing

Display a QR code on your projector so students can jump straight into a live assignment by scanning with their phone. No typing required.

🎓

Student Dashboard

Students log in to their own dashboard to see pending homework, live assignments and access free-choice revision quizzes anytime.

🏆

Leaderboard & Progress

Optional opt-in leaderboard motivates students through friendly competition. Individual progress tracking keeps them focused on their own growth too.

🤖

AI Quiz Generation

Need a quiz on a specific topic that isn't in the library yet? RADevo can generate one in minutes using AI — ready to assign straight away.
